UX-UI / St-Art Project

A responsive web app it is called St-Art and is about contemporary artists and passionate art lovers.

Art lovers can filter their artwork search, find the style and the painting of their desire artist and commission a painting.

Artists can start exhibit in the outfit (Industrial places, Restaurants, Offices etc.), participate in St-Art events, get mentorship and sale artworks worldwide.

UX-UI / InkTank Project

This is an app it is called Ink Tank and it is all about the tattoo industry.

Tattoo lovers can get inspiration for tattoo designs, book online appointment and get updates for the latest news about the tattoo.

Tattoo practitioners and individual artists can build a profile with guidelines and grow their business globally.
